# Round 2 — Facebook DM Drafts (Discovery Conversations)

## Strategy

These are cold DMs to people who commented on Matt's post about finding parameters for Chinese fiber lasers. The goal is NOT to pitch — it's to ask a genuine follow-up question that:
1. Shows you actually read and valued their specific comment
2. Opens a thread about their workflow
3. Positions you as someone building in this space (without selling anything yet)

Keep it under 5 sentences. Match their vibe — these are shop guys, not SaaS buyers.

## Sending Notes

- Send during weekday evenings or Saturday mornings (hobbyists and small shop owners are active then)
- Space them out — don't send all 5 in the same hour (looks bot-like if anyone compares notes)
- If someone replies with even one sentence, match their length and energy — don't escalate to a pitch
- Goal for this round: get 2-3 into a back-and-forth thread. A call invitation comes in message 3 or 4, not message 1
- If they don't reply within a week, leave it — no follow-up on a cold DM that got no response
