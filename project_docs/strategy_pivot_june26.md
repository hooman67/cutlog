# CutLog Strategy Pivot: Focus, Don't Spread

**Date**: 2026-06-26
**Trigger**: r/indiehackers research (see `indiehackers_learnings.md`)

---

## The Pivot (What Changes)

### STOP:
1. Spreading across 5+ channels at surface level
2. Posting promos ("check out my tool")
3. Waiting on Nate Keen as the primary growth lever
4. Asking people to "log cuts" before they've gotten value

### START:
5. Going deep on Reddit + YouTube comments (45 min/day minimum)
6. Solving specific problems in every interaction (mention tool only as source)
7. Treating Nate as a bonus, not the plan
8. Delivering value first, then prompting "did this work? yes/no" (lowest friction)

---

## Daily Routine (Non-Negotiable)

- **15 min**: Find 3-5 "what settings for X?" questions on Reddit/YouTube
- **15 min**: Answer them with actual data from CutLog's DB (include link naturally)
- **15 min**: Check DM replies, respond to Facebook comments, DM new leads from lead-gen post

---

## Weekly Actions

- 1 Reddit post per week (story format, not promo)
- Monitor Facebook lead-gen post comments → DM each commenter
- Create 1 SEO page (long-tail keyword: "best settings for [material] on [machine]")
- Check in with any influencers who replied

---

## Monthly Milestones

- **Week 1-2**: Establish daily Reddit/YouTube presence. Target: 5 helpful answers/day
- **Week 3-4**: Create 5 SEO-optimized pages. Target: organic search traffic starting
- **Month 2**: Product Hunt launch. Target: 100+ signups on launch day
- **Month 2-3**: 20 micro-influencer outreach (500-5K subs). Target: 3-5 video mentions

---

## What Stays the Same

- Facebook DMs continue (they work at 30% reply rate)
- Nate Keen relationship continues (just not as the primary strategy)
- Influencer outreach continues in parallel (but secondary)
- Reddit posting schedule continues (but with story format, not promo)

---

## Success Metrics (30 Days)

- 10+ people who actually TRY a recommendation
- 5+ people who give feedback (too fast/perfect/too slow)
- 50+ new signups from community presence
- First organic search traffic from SEO pages
