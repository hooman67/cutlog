## Sending Strategy & Tone Rules

### Sending Cadence:
| Tier | Timeline | Frequency | Spacing |
|------|----------|-----------|---------|
| **Tier 1** | This week (Tue-Thu) | 1 per day | 24 hours apart |
| **Tier 2** | Next week (staggered) | 1 per day, M-W | Wait for Tier 1 replies first |

### If Someone Replies:
- **Match their energy.** Keep follow-ups short (1-2 sentences).
- **Move to calls fast** if momentum builds. (Don't email-thread forever.)
- **Listen more than pitch.** Questions = engagement.
- **One concrete ask per message.** ("Try it" not "thoughts?")

### If No Reply After 1 Week:
- Send one 1-line follow-up (genuine, not pushy).
- Then leave it. They'll come back if they're interested.

### Tone Rules:
✅ **DO:**
- Mention their specific insight (shows you paid attention)
- Make the ask super concrete
- Own the nerd energy ("literally auto-adjusts" not "features advanced calibration")
- Emoji OK (keeps it light, don't overdo it)
- Self-deprecating humor (OK if it's about the problem, not about them)

❌ **DON'T:**
- Generic opening ("Hey, saw your comment...")
- Vague asks ("Interested?")
- Salesy language ("Game-changing," "revolutionary," "solution")
- Jokes about them (jokes about the problem: fine)
- Ask for feedback before they've tried it

### Message Length:
- **Target: 60-80 words.** If it goes over 100, cut it.
- **One paragraph max** (unless it's multi-line DM with link).
- **Scan-able on mobile.**
